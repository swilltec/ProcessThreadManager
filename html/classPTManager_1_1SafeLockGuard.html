PTManager::SafeLockGuard::SafeLockGuard ( SafeMutex &  m)
explicit

RAII wrapper that acquires a SafeMutex and automatically releases it.

Parameters
mThe SafeMutex to lock
Exceptions
std::runtime_errorif lock acquisition fails

Acquires the mutex in the constructor using the default timeout. Throws exception if lock cannot be acquired, preventing silent failures. Ensures the lock is always released when the guard goes out of scope.
